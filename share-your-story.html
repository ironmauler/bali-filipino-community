🎉 BaliFILs Memory Portal

Your story belongs here.

If you've been part of BaliFILs — as an officer, a member, a family of one, or just someone who came to one gathering and never forgot it — we want to write the next chapter with you. Share your memories, achievements, photos, and the names of the people who made it all happen. We'll turn them into articles on this site.

Open the submission portal What can I share?
How it works

Three steps. That's it.

Sign in with Google so we know it's you, share your story, and we'll celebrate every single contribution.

1

Sign in with Google

One tap. We use Google sign-in so we know who you are and so you can upload photos directly from your device.

2

Tell your story

Pick a memory, type a few sentences, attach photos, give us the year (a guess is fine). It takes about 3 minutes.

3

Get celebrated 🎉

You'll get a thank-you on the spot, and a personal note from BaliFILs admin team. The strongest stories become articles on this site — credited to you, with your permission.

What to share

Anything that matters to you.

If you can't decide where it fits — just pick the closest category. The admin team sorts it later.

📜

BaliFILs history

Founding stories, the earliest gatherings, group-chat origins, first members.

👔

Officer roles

If you served as President, VP, Secretary, Treasurer, Auditor, PRO or Adviser — when, and what you did.

🏆

Personal achievements

Promotions, awards, opening a business, raising your family, finishing school in Bali.

🎉

Past events

Independence Days, Christmas parties, basketball tournaments, sports days, fundraisers, weddings.

📷

Photos & videos

Old albums, screenshots from group chats, photos of past officer rosters, group shots — every image helps.

🙏

Tributes & memorials

Members or officers we've lost, kababayans whose contributions deserve to be remembered.

🤝

Friend nominations

Nominate another kababayan — a notable musician, chef, hospitality leader, mentor. We'll reach out.

💭

Anything else

A funny story, a meaningful moment, a lesson learned. If it mattered to you, it matters to us.

Story ideas

Stuck? Try one of these.

"How I got my KITAS"The agent, the paperwork, the panic, the relief. Someone is going through it right now.

🛠️ For BaliFILs admin team (this section is yours, Jeff)

The portal landing page is ready. To make the actual submission form live, you need to create a Google Form once — about 5 minutes — and then paste its URL into this page. The simplest way is to run the Apps Script below, which sets up the form, response sheet, Drive folder, and confirmation email automatically.

Step 1 — Create the form with one click

  1. Open script.google.com while signed into the balifilipinos@gmail.com account.
  2. Click New project.
  3. Delete the placeholder code and paste the BaliFILs Portal setup script (below).
  4. Click the ▶️ Run button. Approve the permissions when Google asks.
  5. In the script's Execution log at the bottom, you'll see two URLs printed:
    • The public form URL — that's the one to share with members.
    • The response spreadsheet URL — that's your admin view.

Step 2 — Wire the form URL into this page

  1. Open share-your-story.html in any editor.
  2. Find the line that says id="portalLink" (search for that exact text).
  3. Change the href="#admin-setup" to href="YOUR_FORM_URL_HERE" and add target="_blank" rel="noopener".
  4. Save. The "Open the portal" button now links to the live form.

Or just send the form URL to me in chat and I'll wire it.

Step 3 — Your admin workflow

  • Every submission lands in the response spreadsheet automatically — with name, email, date, story, and links to uploaded photos.
  • Each submitter gets an automatic thank-you email from the script.
  • Photos go into a Drive folder titled "BaliFILs Memory Portal — Submissions".
  • To turn a submission into an article: copy its content into a new doc, drop the photos into images/, and write me a quick note in chat — I'll format and publish it.
  • Consider replying personally to every submission within a week. That single follow-up is what makes the portal feel fun, not bureaucratic.

The Apps Script — paste this into script.google.com

/**
 * BaliFILs Memory Portal setup — runs once.
 * Creates: a Google Form, a linked response spreadsheet,
 * a Drive folder for uploaded photos, and an auto-reply email.
 */
function setupCommunityPortal() {
  var FORM_TITLE = "BaliFILs Memory Portal — Share Your Story";
  var FOLDER_NAME = "BaliFILs Memory Portal — Submissions";
  var ADMIN_EMAIL = "balifilipinos@gmail.com";

  // 1. Photo upload folder
  var folder = DriveApp.createFolder(FOLDER_NAME);

  // 2. Create the form
  var form = FormApp.create(FORM_TITLE);
  form.setDescription(
    "Tell us your story — a memory, an achievement, a photo, an event from the past. " +
    "We'll add the strongest stories to BaliFILs website, credited to you (with your permission). " +
    "Salamat for sharing — every submission matters."
  );
  form.setCollectEmail(true);
  form.setRequireLogin(true);            // forces Google sign-in
  form.setAllowResponseEdits(true);
  form.setConfirmationMessage(
    "🎉 Salamat po! Your story is safely with BaliFILs admin team.\n\n" +
    "You'll get a confirmation email shortly, and a personal note from us within a week. " +
    "Mabuhay!"
  );

  // 3. Fields
  form.addTextItem()
      .setTitle("How would you like to be credited?")
      .setHelpText("Your full name, a first name only, or 'anonymous' — your call.")
      .setRequired(true);

  var roleItem = form.addMultipleChoiceItem()
      .setTitle("Your connection to BaliFILs")
      .setChoiceValues([
        "Current officer",
        "Past officer",
        "Current member",
        "Family of a member",
        "Friend of the community",
        "Other"
      ])
      .setRequired(true);

  var typeItem = form.addCheckboxItem()
      .setTitle("What kind of story is this?")
      .setChoiceValues([
        "BaliFILs history / early days",
        "Officer history (names, roles, years)",
        "Personal achievement",
        "Past event recap",
        "Photos / videos",
        "Tribute / memorial",
        "Nomination of another kababayan",
        "Something else"
      ])
      .setRequired(true);

  form.addTextItem()
      .setTitle("Year or approximate date")
      .setHelpText("A guess is fine — 'around 2017' or '2019 Christmas party' both work.");

  form.addTextItem()
      .setTitle("Title of your story")
      .setRequired(true);

  form.addParagraphTextItem()
      .setTitle("Tell us about it")
      .setHelpText("As much or as little as you'd like — we'll edit lightly and check with you before publishing.")
      .setRequired(true);

  form.addTextItem()
      .setTitle("Where in Bali did this happen?")
      .setHelpText("Sanur, Nusa Dua, Kuta, Ubud, Denpasar, etc.");

  form.addTextItem()
      .setTitle("Names of others involved (optional)")
      .setHelpText("Officers, members, friends — we'll reach out to them too.");

  form.addParagraphTextItem()
      .setTitle("Any links?")
      .setHelpText("News articles, Facebook posts, YouTube videos, anything we can use as a source.");

  // Photo upload — needs login (already set above)
  form.addFileUploadItem()
      .setTitle("Upload photos or scanned documents (up to 10)")
      .setHelpText("Drag and drop, or pick from your phone. Photos help a story land.")
      .setMaxFiles(10)
      .setMaxFileSize(50)  // MB total
      .setDestinationDriveFolder(folder.getId());

  form.addMultipleChoiceItem()
      .setTitle("May we publish this on BaliFILs website?")
      .setChoiceValues([
        "Yes — credit me by name",
        "Yes — first name only",
        "Yes — but keep me anonymous",
        "No — for BaliFILs archive only"
      ])
      .setRequired(true);

  form.addParagraphTextItem()
      .setTitle("Anything else for the admin team?")
      .setHelpText("Optional. A note, a thank you, a question.");

  // 4. Link a response spreadsheet
  var ss = SpreadsheetApp.create("BaliFILs Memory Portal — Responses");
  form.setDestination(FormApp.DestinationType.SPREADSHEET, ss.getId());

  // 5. Auto-reply trigger
  ScriptApp.newTrigger("sendCommunityThankYou")
    .forForm(form)
    .onFormSubmit()
    .create();

  // 6. Log URLs
  Logger.log("✅ Form (share this URL with members):  " + form.getPublishedUrl());
  Logger.log("📊 Responses spreadsheet (admin view): " + ss.getUrl());
  Logger.log("📁 Photo upload folder:                " + folder.getUrl());
}

/**
 * Sends a warm thank-you on every submission.
 */
function sendCommunityThankYou(e) {
  try {
    var responses = e.response.getItemResponses();
    var nameGuess = "kababayan";
    var emailAddr = e.response.getRespondentEmail();
    for (var i = 0; i < responses.length; i++) {
      var t = responses[i].getItem().getTitle();
      if (t.indexOf("credited") >= 0) { nameGuess = responses[i].getResponse() || nameGuess; }
    }
    if (!emailAddr) return;
    var subject = "🎉 Salamat po, " + nameGuess + " — your BaliFILs story is with us";
    var body =
      "Hi " + nameGuess + ",\n\n" +
      "Thank you for sharing your story with the Filipinos in Bali. " +
      "We received it safely and will read it carefully.\n\n" +
      "Within a week, one of our admins will personally reply — and if your story " +
      "is a good fit for BaliFILs website, we'll work with you to publish it (credited " +
      "exactly the way you asked).\n\n" +
      "Every submission helps us write our community's history. We are grateful you took the time.\n\n" +
      "Mabuhay,\n" +
      "The Filipinos in Bali admin team\n" +
      "balifilipinos@gmail.com";
    MailApp.sendEmail(emailAddr, subject, body);
  } catch(err) {
    Logger.log("Thank-you email failed: " + err);
  }
}

Tweaks you might want

  • Change the Form's title, description, or thank-you message — edit it directly in the form after running the script.
  • Add a custom header image in the form (Form → Customise theme).
  • Share the response spreadsheet with co-admins so multiple people can triage.
  • To rerun the setup script (e.g., after edits), delete the previously-created form and spreadsheet first, otherwise you'll end up with duplicates.
