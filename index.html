Why we exist

A community that has your back

Moving to Bali is exciting — and full of questions. BaliFILs exists so that no Filipino has to figure it out alone.

Practical guides

Clear, up-to-date answers on visas, work, costs, healthcare, and consular services — written for kababayans.

Real gatherings

Heritage celebrations, potluck lunches, videoke, and meet-ups where you can speak Tagalog and feel at home.

A helping hand

Job leads, trusted referrals, and members who have been through it all and are happy to guide you.

Growing together

Starting in Bali — and building toward a network that serves Filipinos across all of Indonesia.
