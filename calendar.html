Community calendar

A year of bayanihan

BaliFILs monthly programs · June 2026 – January 2027
Monthly themes

Every month, a reason to come together

Each month pairs a celebration with a way to give back — to our kababayan and to the island we call home. Dates and venues are announced in the Facebook group.

Jun 2026
CSR · Filipino community uplift

Philippine Independence Month 🇵🇭

Freedom · Heritage · Unity · Pride

The month of our 128th Araw ng Kalayaan — headlined by the Independence Day dinner celebration on June 12 at Ramayana Hall, Bendega, Renon.

Jul 2026
CSR · Health + charity movement

Fun Run & Wellness Month 🏃

Move for a cause

A community fun run where every kilometer counts — entry donations go to charity, and everyone from sprinters to strollers is welcome.

Aug 2026
CSR · Giving back to our host island

Indonesia Independence Month 🇮🇩

Dirgahayu Indonesia!

We celebrate our host country's independence by giving back: a food donation drive for low-income workers and libreng gupit — free haircuts for the community.

Sep 2026
Activities to be announced

Community Recovery Month 🌿

Bayanihan in action

A month for healing and helping — ideas on the table include a beach or banjar cleanup with local neighbors, a blood donation drive, a mental wellness talk, and mangrove planting. Have a suggestion? Tell us in the group.

Oct 2026
CSR · Fundraising for orphanages

Halloween & Oktoberfest 🎃

Spooky season, Pinoy style

An Oktoberfest Pinoy food festival, a Halloween costume party open to all, a fundraising dinner for orphanages, and trick-or-treat for local kids.

Nov 2026
CSR · Giving back season

Gratitude Month 🤝

Salamat po

"Thank You Workers Day" honoring cleaners, drivers, and staff; a community free meal day; and a pay-it-forward program to keep the kindness moving.

Dec 2026
CSR · Biggest giving month

Christmas Season 🎄

Maligayang Pasko

A Christmas gift drive for children and a community Christmas dinner open to the public — the most Filipino season of all, shared with everyone.

Jan 2027
CSR · Rebuilding + fresh starts

New Year Reset 🌅

Bagong taon, bagong simula

We open the year by doubling down on sports and wellness — more games, more activities, more reasons to get moving together.

Every week, all year

Beyond the monthly programs, BaliFILs plays together weekly: badminton, padel, billiards, basketball, and online chess. Schedules are posted in the Facebook group and WhatsApp.

Be part of every gathering

Dates, venues, and sign-ups are announced first in our Facebook group. Join, turn on notifications, and we'll see you there.
