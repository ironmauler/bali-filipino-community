About

Who we are

Celebrate our heritage. Strengthen our community. Build our future together.

The Filipinos in Bali (BaliFILs) is a network of Filipinos living, working, and building lives on the island of Bali. We are hotel and resort staff, spa therapists, musicians, dive instructors, teachers, business owners, remote workers, families, and friends — kababayans who chose to make Bali home, and who chose not to do it alone.

BaliFILs began the way these things always do: a few Filipinos finding one another, sharing a meal, swapping advice, and deciding to look after each other. Today, that instinct has grown into an organized community with an appointed leadership team, regular gatherings, and — now — this website.

Our mission

To make sure that no Filipino in Bali ever has to feel alone. We do that by connecting kababayans, helping newcomers settle in, sharing reliable information, and celebrating Filipino heritage and identity far from home.

What we do

Connect kababayans

An island-wide network where Filipinos find friends, job leads, housing, and trusted referrals.

Guide newcomers

Practical, researched guides on visas, work, costs, healthcare, and consular services.

Celebrate heritage

Gatherings, fiestas, and heritage events that keep Filipino culture alive in Bali.

Support one another

A community that shows up — for newcomers, for families, and for anyone going through a hard time.

Our values

Bayanihan. We carry each other. Helping a kababayan is not a favour — it is who we are.

Malasakit. Genuine care for one another's wellbeing, beyond what is convenient.

Pagkakaisa. Unity across regions, dialects, jobs, and backgrounds. In Bali, we are simply Filipino.

Integrity. Honest information, trustworthy referrals, and a community newcomers can rely on.

Where we're headed

BaliFILs starts in Bali — but our vision reaches further. The same need for connection, reliable information, and belonging exists for Filipinos in Jakarta, Surabaya, and every other corner of the country. Our long-term goal is to grow from a Bali community into a network that serves Filipinos across all of Indonesia.

This website is built with that future in mind. Bali is our first chapter — not our last.
